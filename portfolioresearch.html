Porfolio Research

http://www.eugeneso.com/

1. The first impression of this website is that its theme is very consistent in all the pages which helps make the transition effects feel more smoothly. It creates an effortless browsing experience for users.

2. The websites contains well-informed projects, which helps explain a lot the designer's works, one thing that a lot of design portfolio lack of. A portfolio should not only showing the visuality of the work but also help the readers know more about the challenges a designer faced and his/her solution to one project.

https://www.marcomarino.design/

1. First impression is that the website goes for minimalist style, which appears not busy for the eyes and pleasant to roll through/revisit. Going with the theme "Less is More" could also mean to focus mainly on effects that helps users get the best browsing experience without spending a lot of time on building a complex template. Meanwhile, The projects he did also shows that he works well with different color palletes.

2. This website is also an example of a well-informed portfolio website, which mostly goes through three of four main points in describing a project: brief, challenges, goals, and solutions.
